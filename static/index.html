Whos Left - Leave No One Behind

Our Idea


Our project aims at solving the most basic problem during times of disaster, i.e., to ensure that nobody is left behind. During the calamity, the disaster management agencies often do not have accurate information on how many people are stuck somewhere and haven’t been taken to the nearest safe place. Our solution is deploying an inexpensive camera module at various buildings such as residences, malls, offices, airports, etc. This doesn’t include functionalities such as thermal imagining due to reasons such as unavailability and opaqueness to such instruments offered by walls. We offer a simple fix to this problem. Our camera module will be placed at the entry and exit points of the buildings such that all the people entering or exiting is accounted for. This data will be sent to a server that maintains the count of the people still inside. However, in a case of emergency, it is a matter of ensuring whether a building has been evacuated or not.
